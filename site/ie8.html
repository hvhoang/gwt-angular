GWT Angular is not supported on IE8

IE8 is an older browser, which does not conform to the modern technology standards. The usage of IE8 has been in steady decline. AngularJS, the framework on which GWT Angular is based, is dropping IE8 support starting their 1.3 release.

In theory, it is possible to fork GWT Angular into two branches, Angular 1.2.x branch and future 1.3.x branch to continue IE8 support in one of the branches, but given that GWT Angular is a one-man operation at this time (which I hope will change in the future), there is really not enough resources to maintain two branches simultaneously.

If anyone is reading this statement, and wish to change it, please join me on Github, and contribute.
